GRIDX is a sub-lib focusing on GRID operations. </br>
The intent of this sub-lib is to not overlap any capability available in databricks H3 native expressions. </br>
GRIDX provides:
<ul>
    <li>H3 expressions that do not exist in databricks H3 expression set. (e.g. h3_kloop)</li>
    <li>H3 internals for building custom expressions</li>
    <li>BNG (british national grid) expressions</li>
    <li>BNG internals for building custom expressions</li>
    <li>Custom Grid internals for building custom expressions</li>
</ul>
